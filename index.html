What do I do?

I am currently in my last year of college at UW Seattle, studying Informatics with concentrations in Human Computer Interaction and Data Science. I think of myself as a design thinker, with active listening skills and a true believer that we can use data to forge a better world for the future generations.

Why did I decide to do this?

"Informatics and Data Science are close to the philosophy of the X1 century, we ask questions and try to solve them with data in form of information to create knowledge. Then we transmit knowledge in different ways like books and most recently on interfaces through visualizations." - Me.

I realized about my interest in user interface design since when I was young I got VERY excited about the new Xbox interface upgrades; I spent hours checking the new features and looking on what I would have done to make it better. Now, coming to Seattle I enrolled myself into Database and information courses in which I developed an interest on how data transformed into information is essential for our day to day problem solving; from getting the fastest way to get home, through checking what to invest in, and even to which player should we bench in our fantasy league.

Who am I outside of work?

I'm an ex football (soccer) player so I go almost every Wednesday and Thursday to play soccer with some other geek friends. I love sports so apart from trying to use my awesome Data Scientist skills on them, I like to watch NBA and American Football on TV, Baseball games at the stadium, and soccer everywhere. I'm an Arsenal, Rayados, Mariners, Trail Blazers, and Seahawks fan. I enjoy running as my primary excersise but ngl my ankles are getting worse every passsing year. Finally, after this almost information overload I just gave you, I love reading about psychology, philosophy, data related topics, and computer science.
